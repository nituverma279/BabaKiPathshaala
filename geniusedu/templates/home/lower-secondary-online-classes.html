Lower secondary online classes

Geniusedu lower secondary online classes develop skills in important subjects including English, Mathematics and Science in a healthy peer learning environment. Our curriculum is flexible with learning objectives. It sets clear learning goals and focuses on developing knowledge and skills in all subjects, providing excellent foundations for the next stage of education. All you need to have is a good internet connection and a quiet place where you will not be disturbed. You will join a small group along with other students at the same level in our friendly classes, led by a Geniusedu teacher. You will be able to access online content before the online class so that you can prepare and understand effectively. We aim to develop digital literacy skills to set you up for a bright future.
