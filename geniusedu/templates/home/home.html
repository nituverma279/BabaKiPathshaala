About Us

Baba ki Pathshaala is an online platform that provides an opportunity for users for their overall development rather than just academics. We ensure this by offering a wide platform including extracurriculars as well as mental health support to the users by offering them psychological help. For ensuring this, we have 5 major sections on our platform:

  1. Academics - Covering the basic education related to classes 1 to 12.
  2. Extra-Curricular - The Arts related to Sketching, Music, Dance, Painting, etc
  3. Competitive/Entrance Exams - Preparing you for exams like JEE, UPSC, SSC, and others.
  4. Professional Courses - To update your knowledge, Re-skill you and pump up your career by live courses on AI, ML, Computer Language, etc.
  5. Mental Health - Very important section, containing an Expert panel of psychologists to support & help you whenever you want.

We aim to develop the skills required for handling real-life situations and to diversify the knowledge areas of users so that when they enter the real world, they have the ability to handle it.

Our highlight features that make us better than others are:

  1. Single platform for all your learning needs like academics, extra curricular, Professional needs, Competitive exam, mental health.
  2. Perfect teacher to student ratio of 1:5.
  3. Expert Panel of psychologists to help you.
  4. Two way communication to your Educator.
