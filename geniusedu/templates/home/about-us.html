Welcome to Eventure

The inception of genius edu began in the year 2009. We inspire students to score with our mind opening and utmost creative techniques that have made us grow stronger every year. Our strength and capability to inspire students and to make them score applying approach the easiest and creative methodologies in learning. A holistic approach helps students to excel in examinations and also develop critical and high order thinking skills which are in accordance to Ministry of Education Blueprints that focuses on KBAT/HOTS-based curriculum.

Our core is to strive towards bringing and nurturing students not only academically but also to become better citizens. Hence, strategies and expertise are passed on to students, teachers and parents in our highly rated academic and self-development programmers, seminars and workshops for e.g. KBAT camps, Latihan Dalam Perkhidmatan (LADAP) and many others to lead and build a highly spirited nation.

With this determination, we believe that our efforts will make each and every learning moment of our students to see and experience that we are different from the rest. The milestone of our achievements is our students, parents, teachers and Ministry of Education who have welcomed and continuously supported and given us the opportunity for this successful journey throughout the years. We are proud to mention and happy that you are part of our success stories.
